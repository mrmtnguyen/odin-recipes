Pho

Description

Beef pho is a popular Vietnamese noodle soup that typically consists of a clear broth, rice noodles, thinly sliced beef, and various herbs and spices.

Ingredients

  • Rice noodle
  • Beef balls
  • Beef bones

Steps

  1. Par-boil beef bones for 10 minutes
  2. Remove bones and put into a large pot with 2 litres of water
  3. Boil rice noodles for 15 seconds and rinse with cold water
