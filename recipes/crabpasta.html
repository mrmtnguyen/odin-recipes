Crab Pasta

Description

Crab pasta is a dish that typically consists of cooked pasta noodles tossed with a sauce made from crab meat, herbs, and other ingredients such as garlic, lemon, cream, and olive oil. The crab meat is often saut√©ed with the other ingredients to create a flavorful sauce that coats the noodles. Crab pasta can be served as a main dish or as a side dish and is often garnished with fresh herbs and grated cheese.

Ingredients

  • Crab
  • Spaghetti Pasta
  • Cream

Steps

  1. Boil crab and remove meat
  2. Boil spaghetti until al dente
  3. Mix spaghetti and crab meat into the cream in a medium sauce pan
