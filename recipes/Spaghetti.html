Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a type of long, thin pasta that is popular in Italian cuisine. It is made from wheat flour and water, and is typically boiled until it is soft but still slightly firm to the bite. Spaghetti can be served with a variety of sauces, such as tomato sauce, meat sauce, pesto, or garlic and olive oil. It can also be topped with grated cheese or other garnishes. Spaghetti is a versatile and popular dish that is enjoyed around the world.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Ground beef
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef
  2. Boil pasta until al dente
  3. Top with parmesan cheese
